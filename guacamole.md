**Ingredients:**

* 2 avocados
* 1 lime
* 2 tsp salt
* jalepeno to taste
* cilantro to taste
* 1/2 onion

**Instructions:**

* cut and mash avocados
* chop onion
* chop the jalepeno
* chop the cilantro
* squeeze lime
* add salt
and mix well
